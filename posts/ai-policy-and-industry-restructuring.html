AI Policy and Industry Restructuring

November 27, 2025

The AI landscape is rapidly evolving with major policy initiatives and industry shifts reshaping how companies and governments approach artificial intelligence infrastructure and development.

Trump Launches 'Genesis Mission' to Build Integrated National AI Platform

President Trump signed an executive order launching the "Genesis Mission," a coordinated national effort to build the American Science and Security Platform, an integrated AI system harnessing federal supercomputers and scientific datasets.

This initiative signals a major, federally-funded push to accelerate U.S. scientific discovery and technological leadership by treating AI infrastructure as a critical national asset, potentially leading to breakthroughs in energy, health, and manufacturing.

HP to Cut Up to 6,000 Jobs by 2028, Ramps Up AI Efforts

HP Inc. announced plans to reduce its workforce by 4,000 to 6,000 employees by fiscal 2028 as part of a restructuring plan to cut costs and shift investment toward AI-driven opportunities.

This is a clear example of how major corporations are reallocating resources, trading traditional roles for new AI-focused positions, underscoring the technology's transformative and disruptive impact on the global job market.

Nvidia Stock Falls Amid Report of Google and Meta AI Chip Deal Talks

Nvidia's stock dipped following a report that Google and Meta are in talks for a multi-billion dollar deal to supply each other with custom-designed AI chips, reducing reliance on Nvidia's hardware.

This move by two of the largest AI players highlights the intense, multi-billion dollar race to control the underlying AI infrastructure, suggesting a future where custom-built chips challenge the current dominance of a single supplier.
