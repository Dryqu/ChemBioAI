Turn a Paper Business Card into a Phone Contact with AI

December 10, 2025 • By Yi Qu

We all collect business cards…and then forget to type them into our phones.

Here is a simple way to let AI do the boring part for you.

You only need three things:

  • Your phone
  • A business card
  • An AI chat app (for example, ChatGPT)

Step 1 - Take a photo of the card

Use your phone camera and take a clear photo of the business card:

  • Put it on a flat surface
  • Make sure the text is sharp and not blurry
  • Capture the whole card in the picture

Step 2 - Ask AI to read the card

Open your AI chat app and upload the photo.

Then type a simple message, for example:

"Please extract the contact info from this business card and create a VCF (vCard) file so I can add it to my phone."

The AI should:

  • Read the name, job title, company
  • Read the email, phone number, website, address
  • Generate the file as a .vcf download.

Step 3 - Save the vCard to your phone

On iPhone

  1. Tap the .vcf file.
  2. Your iPhone will show a preview of the contact.
  3. Tap "Create New Contact" (or "Add to Existing Contact" if you want to update someone).
  4. Check the fields and tap Save.

On Android

  1. Tap the .vcf file.
  2. Choose Contacts if asked which app to use.
  3. Confirm you want to import the contact and save.

Step 4 - Optional: Just copy and paste

If you don't want a file, you can ask:

"Please give me the contact info in this format: Name: Company: Title: Phone: Email: Website: Address:"

Then you can copy-paste each line into your Contacts app.

Why this is useful

  • No manual typing
  • Fewer mistakes in email or phone numbers
  • Easy to process many cards after a conference or meeting

Next time someone hands you a business card, you don't have to let it sit in your wallet.

Snap a photo, let AI read it, tap once, and the contact is in your phone.
