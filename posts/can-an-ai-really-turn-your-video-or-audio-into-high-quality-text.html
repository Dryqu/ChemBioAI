Can an AI Really Turn Your Video or Audio Into High-Quality Text?

December 10, 2025 • By Yi Qu

Many people still don’t know this: with Gemini 3, I can drag in a video or audio file and get a clean transcript in just a few moments.

Turning Video Into Text With Gemini 3

I take one of my recent videos, drag it into Gemini 3, and simply say:

“Please give me a full transcript of this video.”

Gemini 3 then turns the whole video into text, and the accuracy is very high.

Compared with many traditional speech-to-text tools I used before, there are far fewer mistakes.

Because it’s powered by a large language model, Gemini 3 understands the meaning of what I say. So even if I mix Chinese and English in the same sentence, it still works very well.

Timestamps and Subtitles in Any Language

Sometimes I ask:

“Please give me subtitles with timestamps.”

Gemini 3 can create a subtitle-style transcript with time codes already added.

This works for Chinese, English, and many other languages, so it’s very useful when I want to upload a video with captions.

From Phone Voice Memos to Clean Notes

It also works with audio recorded on my phone.

For example, I can upload a voice memo and ask Gemini 3 to:

  • transcribe the audio
  • detect different speakers
  • label who is speaking

If there are two people, Gemini 3 can often tell there are two speakers.

Then I can say something like:

“I am the person giving suggestions. My name is Zara. Please label my parts.”

Gemini 3 updates the transcript, and I can then ask it to fix the subtitles or turn the transcript into a clean article or outline.

A Few Limits to Keep in Mind

I use this Gemini 3 workflow a lot to turn videos into text.

From my own experience, it works best when the file is under 30–40 minutes.

If the video is very long or very large, it may be less stable, and I might need to cut it into shorter parts.

Still, for most daily videos, meeting recordings, and voice memos, Gemini 3 is more than enough — and it saves me a huge amount of time turning spoken content into ready-to-use text.
