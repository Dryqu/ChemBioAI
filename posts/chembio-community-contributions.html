Contribute ChemBio AI Case Notes

Contribute · Dec 7, 2025

ChemBio AI is a community of scientists trading practical notes on how AI lands in regulated labs and field programs. We welcome concise, experiment-first stories that others can reproduce.

What to submit

  • Case studies with objective measures (throughput, error rates, turnaround time).
  • Templates for prompts, validation checklists, or data review workflows.
  • Lessons from pilots—what worked, what failed, and why.

How to share

  • Email us at chembioaiinsights@gmail.com with a short abstract.
  • Include links to supporting materials (sanitized notebooks, screenshots, or diagrams).
  • Note whether we can attribute the work or need to anonymize details.

Every contribution is reviewed for clarity and reproducibility. Together we can help peers deploy AI responsibly across pharma and agtech programs.
