AI for Clinical Candidate Selection

Pharma · Nov 24, 2025

Discovery teams are using AI to narrow the list of viable clinical candidates by uniting assay evidence, predicted ADME profiles, and safety signals. The goal: fewer surprises before first-in-human studies.

What teams are doing

  • LLMs summarize dossiers across tox, DMPK, and manufacturability so leads can be compared on a like-for-like basis.
  • Predictive models estimate exposure and clearance for different dosing schemes to prioritize tolerable ranges.
  • Simulation helps explore combination therapies and sequence effects before expensive in vivo work.

Keeping rigor high

Each model recommendation is paired with documented assumptions and validation runs. Scientists keep the loop tight with orthogonal assays and clear decision logs so candidates advance with fewer unknowns.
